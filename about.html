About

Born in 2001 in the northern urban outskirts of Saint Petersburg, Russia into a family of intellectual snobs. Changed 2 kindergartens, 3 schools, 1 country and a multitude interests. I did judo, danced modern disco, learnt English, physics, math, graphic design and social skills.

When I was 14, I moved to Amsterdam, Netherlands, which resulted in some inner turmoil. I went to an international school, DENISE, and Berlage Lyceum prior to becoming a student at the TU Delft/Leiden University Life Science & Technology joint Degree. I also work at TERTIUS Gallery in Amsterdam. I currently live in The Hague.

This website is a showcase of all work - creative or not - I have created in my life. Sometimes with additional commentary. Please enjoy and share if you like something. It would highly be appreciated to hear your feedback. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact me at velli.maxim@gmail.com
